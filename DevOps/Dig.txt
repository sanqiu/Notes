====== Dig ======
Created Friday 12 September 2014

dig @server name type
eg: dig @89.96.70.60 sunricher.com mx

+[-]TCP
+time=N  timeout
+short  verbose>terse
-t type[A,TXT,MX,SIG]
Network:Nslookup -q **type**
